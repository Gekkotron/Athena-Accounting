# Dashboard

**Status:** draft — content coming.

## What this page will cover

The dashboard is where Athena shows you your money at a glance: a
balance chart with a calendar X-axis, a category donut, an insights
panel with anomaly flags, an optional Sankey view, and a budgets
summary. This page will walk through each widget, the account and date
filters, and the balance-checkpoint diamonds that flag drift from your
bank statements.
